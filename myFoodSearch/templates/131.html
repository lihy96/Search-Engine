Zeera rice or Jeera rice is an Indian dish consisting of rice and cumin seeds.[1] This rice dish is known as "Zeera rice" in Pakistan. It is a popular dish in North India, an everyday rice dish.[2] It is easy to prepare unlike a biryani. Jeera is the Hindi word for cumin seeds.[3]

The ingredients used are rice, cumin seeds, vegetable oil, onions and coriander leaves.
