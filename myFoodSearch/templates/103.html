Rice bread is a type of bread that is made from rice flour rather than wheat flour.[1] Being gluten free,[2] it will not cause adverse reactions for people with gluten intolerance.

The Vietnamese banh mi (baguette) is traditionally made with a mixture of wheat and rice flour or sometimes the latter, resulting in an airy, crispy texture.[3]

In 2001 a study group at Yamagata University's Department of Engineering made use of foam molding technology to produce rice bread made from 100% rice flour.[4][5]
